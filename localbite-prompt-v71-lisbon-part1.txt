# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Lisbon's food scene is concentrated in Chiado/Bairro Alto, Príncipe Real,
# Santos, Mouraria, and the newer creative hubs of Beato and Marvila.
# Alfama is tourist-heavy but has genuine local restaurants.
# LX Factory (Alcântara) is the design/food market district.
# Intendente has emerged as a local neighbourhood dining area.
# Belém is worth including for its cultural restaurants despite being further west.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Chiado, Bairro Alto, Príncipe Real, Santos, Mouraria
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Alfama, Intendente, Beato, Marvila, Alcântara, Graça
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Belém, Campo de Ourique, Amoreiras, Benfica

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
  Chiado, Bairro Alto, Príncipe Real, Santos, Mouraria, Alfama,
  Intendente, Beato, Marvila, Alcântara (LX Factory area), Graça,
  Belém, Campo de Ourique, Amoreiras, Anjos, Arroios

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_EXCLUDE:
  Cascais (separate municipality 30km west), Sintra (separate municipality),
  Setúbal (separate city), Almada (across the Tagus — separate municipality),
  Costa da Caparica, Sesimbra

# ── SOURCE STRATEGY ──────────────────────────────────────────
# Lisbon has strong PT-language food press and a significant EN expat/travel
# food writing scene. Both-pool is achievable with independent PT + EN sources.
# Time Out Lisboa has a commercial conflict — they operate Time Out Market Lisboa
# food hall. Include with ⚠ coi flag but do not reject outright.
# Mesa Marcada (Miguel Pires) is the leading independent PT food critic.
# Observador has strong named food writing in PT.

PHASE_0_SOURCES:
  - mesaMarcada.com (PT — independent food critic Miguel Pires, top PT source)
  - observador.pt (PT — quality digital newspaper, food section with named writers)
  - timeout.com/lisbon (EN/PT — ⚠ COI: operates Time Out Market Lisboa)
  - publico.pt (PT — quality newspaper, food and culture section)

SECONDARY_SOURCES:
  - expedia-style.com or similar EN food travel writers covering Lisbon
  - culinarybackstreets.com (EN — food travel platform, search Lisbon features)
  - eater.com (EN — search Lisbon guide if exists, verify named author)
  - guiarepsol.com (ES — Repsol guide, search Lisbon entries)
  - dn.pt (PT — Diário de Notícias, lifestyle and food section)

COMMERCIAL_CONFLICTS_NOTED:
  Time Out Lisboa — operates Time Out Market Lisboa food hall.
  Flag with ⚠ coi in writer_profile. Do not reject — include transparently.
