IMPORTANT — FREE TAPAS CULTURE:
Granada has a strong free tapas tradition — bars serve a free
tapa with every drink. Many "best tapas in Granada" articles
are tourist-facing roundups of the free tapa bars near the
Alhambra. Prioritise sources that cover the full restaurant
scene including Albayzín, Realejo, Zaidín, and Beiro — not
just the tourist centre around Plaza Nueva and Navas.
