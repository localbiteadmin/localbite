# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Valencia's food identity is defined by rice dishes (paella, arrós al forn,
# arròs negre) and horchata. El Carmen (Barrio del Carmen) is the historic
# dining core. Ruzafa is the creative neighbourhood dining hub.
# Cabanyal is the former fishing village turned dining destination near the beach.
# The Eixample (Valencia's, not Barcelona's) is the main commercial dining area.
# Benimaclet is the university-area neighbourhood with local character.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: El Carmen, Ruzafa, Cabanyal, Eixample
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Benimaclet, Jesús, Quatre Carreres, Extramurs
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Poblats Marítims, Campanar, La Saïdia, Patraix

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
