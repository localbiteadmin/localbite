Palermitan food identity: Palermo is one of Italy's great street
food cities. The pipeline should target:
  - Street food markets (Ballarò, Vucciria, Capo) — arancine,
    panelle, sfincione, pani câ meusa (spleen sandwich)
  - Seafood restaurants (tonno, pesce spada, sarde a beccafico)
  - Modern Sicilian cuisine (Arab-Norman culinary heritage)
  - Casual tratorie serving pasta con le sarde, caponata
  - Cannoli and cassata from historic pastry shops

Bagheria exclusion: Bagheria is consistently grouped with Palermo
in food media but is a separate municipality. Any restaurant
described as being "in Bagheria", "outside Palermo in Bagheria",
or "near Palermo in the province" must be excluded regardless of
recommendation. The two starred restaurants (Lìmù, I Pupi) are both
in Bagheria — the bounding box should catch these, but apply
editorial rejection as well.

Both-pool requirements: Culinary Backstreets (EN) + any qualifying
IT source from Phase 1 would form a both-pool pair. Different
publishers, different languages — qualifies.
