# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Bilbao's pintxos culture is centred in Casco Viejo (old town) on Calle del Licenciado
# Poza and the streets around Plaza Nueva. Ensanche (Abando) is the bourgeois
# dining district with higher-end restaurants. Indautxu has strong neighbourhood
# dining. Deusto (university area) has casual dining. San Mamés is the stadium area.
# Basque (Euskara) language sources are valid primary sources alongside ES.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Casco Viejo, Ensanche, Indautxu
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: San Mamés, Deusto, Amézola, Basurto
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Rekalde, Otxarkoaga, Txurdinaga, Begoña

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
  Casco Viejo, Ensanche (Abando), Indautxu, San Mamés, Deusto,
  Amézola, Basurto, Rekalde, Begoña, Santutxu

  Getxo (separate municipality, Las Arenas — different food scene),

  Vitoria-Gasteiz (separate city), Guernica/Gernika,
  Barakaldo, Sestao (separate municipalities in Greater Bilbao metro)
