Italian-language sources: Bologna has strong local food media.
Phase 1 should search for:
  "migliori ristoranti Bologna critico gastronomico blog 2024 2025"
  "dove mangiare Bologna guida 2024 2025"
  "trattorie osterie Bologna consigliate giornalista 2025"
  Named IT Bologna food critics and bloggers

Bolognese food identity: Bologna is called "La Grassa" (the fat one)
for good reason. The pipeline should target:
  - Traditional tagliatelle al ragù bolognese (not spaghetti bolognese)
  - Tortellini in brodo di cappone
  - Lasagne al forno (green pasta, béchamel, ragù)
  - Mortadella (at the Quadrilatero market)
  - Natural wine bars and modern osterie
  - Restaurants in the Quadrilatero market area

Imola warning: San Domenico (2★) is frequently described as
"a Bologna restaurant" by English-language travel media because
Imola is in the province of Bologna. It is 35km from the city.
The bounding box will exclude it geocoding-wise, but any source
that mentions it as a "Bologna restaurant" must have that entry
rejected at extraction stage.

Both-pool requirements: Katie Parla (EN) from Phase 0 + any
qualifying IT-language source from Phase 1 or the CiboToday/
Gambero Rosso blog would form a both-pool pair.
