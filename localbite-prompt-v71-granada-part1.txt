# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Granada's food identity is defined by free tapas culture — order a drink,
# get a tapa. Albaicín (UNESCO) is the historic Moorish quarter with cave bars.
# Realejo (former Jewish quarter) has quality restaurants. Centro is the
# main commercial and tapas bar area. Sacromonte is the flamenco cave district.
# La Zubia and Armilla are outside the city core — exclude.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Centro, Albaicín, Realejo
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Sacromonte, Zaidín, Genil, Chana
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Beiro, Ronda, Cartuja

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
  Centro, Albaicín, Realejo (El Campillo / San Matías area),
  Sacromonte, Zaidín, Genil, Chana, Beiro, Ronda, Cartuja

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_EXCLUDE:
  La Zubia (separate municipality), Armilla (separate municipality),
  Ogíjares, Cenes de la Vega, Sierra Nevada (ski resort 30km away),
  Motril (separate city on coast)
