PHASE 3 — ASSEMBLE, SCORE, AND REVIEW
════════════════════════════════════════

Read ./localbite-porto-working.json as input.

STEP 1 — Deduplicate and merge
Combine all entries. Report merge count.

STEP 2 — Assign confidence tier

TIER A — Auto-approve:
- 2 or more independent sources, OR
- Both-pool: ≥1 EN source AND ≥1 PT source, editorially
  independent AND from different publishers.
  NOTE: Same-publisher cross-language editions (e.g. Time Out EN
  and Time Out PT) do NOT qualify as independent agreement and
  do NOT produce a both-pool entry.

TIER B — Review recommended:
Primary single-source:
- Good fetch AND quote ≥15 words AND ≥1 specific detail

Secondary or conflicted single-source:
- Good fetch AND quote ≥15 words AND ≥2 specific details

TIER C — Auto-reject:
- Partial/failed fetch, single source
- Quote under 15 words or no specific details
- Outside geographic boundary

STEP 2B — PUBLISHER CONCENTRATION CHECK
After tier assignment:

For each unique publisher count:
- Total articles from this publisher in Tier A + Tier B
- Percentage of each language pool held by this publisher

If any publisher holds 2+ articles OR 50%+ of either language
pool's sources:
- Flag: [publisher-concentration] [publisher] — [X] articles,
  [Y]% of [language] pool
- Check whether diversity gate PT requirement (2 distinct
  publishers) is met
- If not met: run up to 4 supplementary searches for an
  independent PT source from a different publisher
- If still not met after 4 searches: document as structural
  characteristic and proceed

Report:
PUBLISHER CONCENTRATION
[Publisher]: [X] articles, [Y]% of [language] pool →
flagged / not flagged / resolved

SOURCE CONCENTRATION CAP (article-level):
If any single article exceeds 30% of total Tier A + Tier B
single-source entries, rank and auto-reject lowest until cap met.
