# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Barcelona has three primary food languages: Spanish (ES), Catalan (CA), and English (EN).
# CA-language sources are primary — Beteve.cat, ElNacional.cat, Ara Mengem are key.
# Eixample (Esquerra and Dreta) is the densest restaurant district.
# Gràcia has the strongest neighbourhood dining identity.
# El Born/Sant Pere is the tapas and craft food hub.
# Poble Sec and Sant Antoni have become serious dining neighbourhoods.
# Barceloneta is the seafood and paella strip (tourist-heavy — verify quality).
# Note: Time Out Barcelona CA and ES editions have shared editorial — do not
# count as two independent sources.
