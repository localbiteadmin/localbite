MESTRE EXCLUSION — READ BEFORE ANY EXTRACTION:
Mestre is frequently described as "Venice" by travel media because
it is administratively part of the Comune di Venezia. Any restaurant
accessible by car, bus, or tram (not boat) is in Mestre/terraferma
and must be excluded. All Venice island restaurants require arrival
by vaporetto, water taxi, or foot across the Rialto/Accademia bridges.
Apply this as an editorial test at extraction stage.

Italian-language sources: Phase 1 should search for:

Venetian food identity: Venice has a distinct food culture.
The pipeline should target:
  - Cicchetti (Venetian tapas, served at bacari wine bars)
  - Bacari (traditional wine bars with small plates)
  - Sarde in saor (sweet-sour sardines)
  - Baccalà mantecato (whipped salt cod)
  - Risotto al nero di seppia (squid ink risotto)
  - Fegato alla veneziana (calves' liver with onions)
  - Seafood from the Rialto market

Overcrowding note: Venice suffers from extreme tourist pressure.
Sources that explicitly recommend "where locals eat" or restaurants
away from the tourist circuit (Cannaregio, Castello, Giudecca,
outer islands) should be prioritised over Piazza San Marco area.

are both confirmed as Phase 0 sources but they are the same language.
For both-pool, at least one IT-language source is needed from Phase 1.
