# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Córdoba's food scene is concentrated in the historic Casco Antiguo/Judería
# around the Mezquita, and the modern Centro. San Basilio is the famous
# patios neighbourhood. Ciudad Jardín and El Brillante are residential dining areas.
# Córdoba is known for salmorejo (thick cold tomato soup), flamenquín, and
# rabo de toro (oxtail stew) — pipeline should surface these local dishes.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Casco Antiguo, Judería, Centro
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: San Basilio, El Brillante, Ciudad Jardín, Sector Sur
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Levante, Poniente Norte, Zumbacón

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
  Casco Antiguo, Judería (Jewish quarter), Centro, San Basilio,
  El Brillante, Ciudad Jardín, Sector Sur, Miraflores
