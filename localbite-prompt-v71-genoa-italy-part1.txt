Genovese food identity: Genoa has one of Italy's great street food
and working-class food cultures. The pipeline should target:
  - Focaccia genovese (the defining snack, sold at every bakery)
  - Focaccia di Recco col formaggio (note: Recco is outside city)
  - Farinata (chickpea flatbread)
  - Pesto alla genovese (birthplace of pesto)
  - Trattorie in the caruggi (medieval alleyways)
  - Seafood from the Porto Antico area
  - Cappon magro (elaborate Ligurian seafood salad)
  - Zimino di ceci (chickpea and chard stew)

Portofino contamination: Portofino restaurants appear constantly
in "Genoa" food coverage. Apply firm bounding box rejection —
Portofino is accessible only by boat or narrow mountain road and
is clearly a separate destination from Genoa city.

Both-pool requirements: No Phase 0 sources confirmed. The pipeline
must find at least one qualifying EN source AND one qualifying IT
source via Phase 1. Both must be different publishers, different
languages. The publish threshold (≥10R) may be harder to reach
without Phase 0 sources — Phase 1 should be aggressive.
