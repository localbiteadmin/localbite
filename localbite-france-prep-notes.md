## French Regional Newspaper Ownership Map

### EBRA (Est Bourgogne Rhône Alpes)
Owner: Crédit Mutuel banking group
Papers: Le Progrès (Lyon), DNA/Dernières Nouvelles d'Alsace (Strasbourg),
        L'Alsace (Mulhouse), Le Dauphiné Libéré (Grenoble), Le Bien Public (Dijon),
        L'Est Républicain (Nancy)

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- Le Progrès (Lyon) and DNA (Strasbourg) are different cities — no conflict.
- Within Lyon: do NOT use Le Progrès AND another EBRA paper as independent sources.
- Within Strasbourg: do NOT use DNA AND L'Alsace as independent sources.
- EBRA ownership is a bank (Crédit Mutuel) — NO food industry COI.

### Groupe Sud Ouest
Owner: Groupe Rossel (Belgian media group)
Papers: Sud Ouest (main paper covering Bordeaux and SW France incl. Bayonne/Biarritz)
        Sud Ouest Bordeaux edition, Sud Ouest Bayonne edition

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- RISK: Sud Ouest covers both Bordeaux AND Biarritz/Bayonne.
- Within each city, Sud Ouest as one source is fine.
- Do NOT claim publisher independence between a Bordeaux Sud Ouest article and
  a Biarritz/Bayonne Sud Ouest article — SAME publisher.
- Within a single city: one Sud Ouest source acceptable.

### La Dépêche du Midi Group
Owner: La Dépêche Group (Baylet family)
Papers: La Dépêche du Midi (Toulouse area), La Nouvelle République des Pyrénées (Tarbes),
        Centre-Presse (Rodez), Le Journal de Millau
Possible: Midi Libre (Montpellier) — ownership unverified, MUST check before pipeline.

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- If Midi Libre is owned by La Dépêche Group: do NOT use both La Dépêche (Toulouse source)
  and Midi Libre (Montpellier source) as independent publishers IN THE SAME CITY PACK.
  (Different city packs are fine — these are different cities.)
- Within Toulouse: La Dépêche du Midi as one source is acceptable.
- Within Montpellier: Midi Libre as one source is acceptable.
- Cross-city publisher overlap does not affect within-city publisher independence.

### Nice-Matin Group / Groupe Nice-Matin
Owner: Independent (co-operative structure)
Papers: Nice-Matin, Var-Matin, Nice-Presse

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- Nice-Matin and Nice-Presse (where Jacques Gantié is) are part of the same group.
- Within Nice: Nice-Matin and Nice-Presse count as ONE publisher.
  Do not use both as independently-sourced publishers for CPs.
- Gantié's Table Libre (at Nice-Presse) is the target. If Nice-Matin also has
  named food coverage, flag as same publisher and count as one source.

### Groupe Ouest-France
Owner: SIPA (Société indépendante de presses et d'associations) — truly independent
Papers: Ouest-France (national-regional), Presse Océan (Nantes)

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- Within Nantes: Presse Océan and Ouest-France Nantes edition are SAME publisher.
  Count as one source.

### La Provence
Owner: CMA-CGM (shipping conglomerate, via media subsidiary)
Papers: La Provence (Marseille regional)

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- CMA-CGM is a shipping company — NOT a food industry COI. Acceptable source.
- Pierre Psaltis was La Provence critic for 20 years but now runs Le Grand Pastis independently.
  Le Grand Pastis and La Provence are different publishers — both acceptable if both found.
- Within Marseille: La Provence + Le Grand Pastis = two independent publishers. ✓

### National Publications (Paris-based)
Le Monde (owned by Groupe Le Monde), Le Figaro (Dassault/Groupe Figaro),
Télérama (Groupe Le Monde), Le Parisien (Groupe LVMH/Lagardère), Libération

IMPACT ON LOCALBITE:
- Le Monde and Télérama: SAME parent group — count as one publisher within a city.
- Le Figaro and Figaroscope: same publisher — count as one.
- Le Parisien Weekend: acceptable.

## Geocode.js Notes

France addresses: French street address format is:
  [number] [rue/avenue/boulevard/place] [name], [postcode] [city]

Known false positive patterns to watch:
- "Rue de [name]" — street name matches. Apply existing geocode false positive rules.
- "Place de [name]" — square name matches. Apply existing rules.
- "Avenue [name]" — avenue name matches. Apply existing rules.
- These patterns are similar to Italian "Via" patterns (Fix 3b) — monitor.

New Fix 3 needed if false positives appear at scale: Add French street prefix patterns
(Rue, Avenue, Boulevard, Place, Allée, Impasse) to NON_RESTAURANT_PATTERNS in geocode.js.
