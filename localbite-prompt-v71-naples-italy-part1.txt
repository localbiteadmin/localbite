DISAMBIGUATION ENFORCEMENT — READ BEFORE ANY SEARCH:
All queries in Phase 1 MUST use "Napoli" (Italian) or "Naples Italy"
(English). NEVER use bare "Naples". This is the single most important
instruction for this pipeline. Contamination with Naples, Florida
results is severe and confirmed. The search log should reflect this.

Neapolitan food identity: Naples is the birthplace of pizza. The
pipeline should target:
  - Pizzerias (Neapolitan pizza — the authentic style)
  - Seafood restaurants (Posillipo, Mergellina seafront)
  - Frittura / street food (cuoppo, sfogliatelle, frittatine)
  - Classic Neapolitan trattorie (pasta e patate, ragù napoletano,
    genovese di cipolla, sartù di riso)
  - Restaurants in the Centro Storico residential neighbourhoods

Italian-language sources: Phase 1 should search for:
  "migliori ristoranti Napoli critico gastronomico blog 2024 2025"
  "Napoli dove mangiare guida gastronomica 2025"
  "ristoranti Napoli quartieri 2024 2025 raccomandati"
  Named IT Naples food writers beyond Luciano Pignataro

Both-pool requirements: Katie Parla (EN) from Phase 0 + Luciano
Pignataro (IT) from Phase 0 = immediate both-pool potential if
they recommend the same restaurants. Different publishers, different
languages — qualifies.

Province contamination: If any source lists restaurants in Ischia,
Capri, Amalfi, or the Sorrento peninsula as "Naples restaurants",
these must be rejected. The bounding box will catch geocoding errors
but the extraction step must also apply editorial rejection for any
restaurant outside Naples city regardless of how the source frames it.
