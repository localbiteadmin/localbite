Italian-language sources: Modena has strong local food coverage
given its Emilian food identity. Phase 1 should search for:
  "migliori ristoranti Modena trattorie osterie 2024 2025"
  "dove mangiare Modena guida gastronomica 2025"
  Named IT Modena food writers and local food bloggers
  Luciano Pignataro may have covered Modena — check.

Modenese food identity: Modena is known globally for Osteria
Francescana but locals eat very differently. The pipeline should
target non-starred restaurants serving:
  - Tortellini in brodo (the Modenese version)
  - Tagliatelle al ragù modenese
  - Tigelle and gnocco fritto (local flatbreads)
  - Lambrusco wine pairings
  - Aceto Balsamico Tradizionale di Modena DOP in cooking
  - Cotechino and zampone (local cured meats)
  - Sandwiches at Bar Schiavoni in Mercato Albinelli

Non-starred restaurant focus: This city has a once-in-a-generation
fine dining restaurant at the top (Osteria Francescana) but the
real food culture is in unpretentious trattorie and markets.
The pipeline should actively seek these out via Phase 1.

Province contamination: Maranello (Ferrari/Bottura's Cavallino),
Rubiera, Nonantola, and other Modena province municipalities will
appear in search results as "Modena restaurants." Reject these
at extraction stage if outside city bounding box.

Both-pool requirements: Katie Parla (EN) from Phase 0 + any
qualifying IT source from Phase 1 would form a both-pool pair.
The Emilia-Romagna food media ecosystem is well-developed — good
prospects for finding a named IT critic via Phase 1.
