Milanese food identity: Milan is known for risotto alla Milanese
(saffron), cotoletta, ossobuco, panettone, Aperol Spritz birthplace
(Campari). Restaurants in the neo-trattoria movement (Trippa etc.) are
prime targets. The Navigli canal district and Isola neighbourhood are
active food areas. Chinese food scene (Sarpi area) is internationally
recognised.
