# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Marrakesh divides clearly between the ancient Medina and the French-built
# Gueliz/Hivernage (Ville Nouvelle). Jemaa el-Fna is the iconic central square.
# Kasbah and Mellah are the southern medina quarters.
# Gueliz is the modern dining hub with wine-serving restaurants.
# Palmeraie is the luxury resort area — exclude unless strong local writer coverage.
# Riad restaurants are extremely common — apply RIAD_RULE.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Medina, Jemaa el-Fna, Gueliz, Hivernage
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Kasbah, Mellah, Bab Doukkala, Mouassine
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Palmeraie, Targa, Ménara
