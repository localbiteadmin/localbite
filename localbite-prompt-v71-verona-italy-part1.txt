Veronese food identity: Verona is the capital of one of Italy's
great wine regions. The pipeline should target:
  - Osterie and trattorias in the centro storico
  - Restaurants pairing local Valpolicella, Amarone, Soave wines
  - Traditional dishes: pastissada de caval (horse meat stew),
    risotto all'Amarone, pearà (pepper sauce), lesso e pearà
  - Wine bars (enoteche) in the historic centre
  - Restaurants near Piazza Erbe and Piazza dei Signori

Romeo and Juliet warning: Verona is heavily touristed due to its
Shakespeare association. Sources that explicitly recommend restaurants
away from the tourist circuit are to be prioritised. Avoid extracting
restaurants that writers describe as "tourist traps" or "near the
Arena" if positioned as tourist destinations.

Province contamination: Valpolicella wine estates and Lake Garda
restaurants frequently appear in Verona food guides as "day trips
from Verona" or "in the Verona area." Any restaurant requiring a
car to reach from Verona city centre should be treated as outside
the bounding box unless confirmed to be within the Comune di Verona.
