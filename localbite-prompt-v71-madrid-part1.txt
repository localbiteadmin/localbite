# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Madrid's food scene is the largest and most diverse in Spain.
# Malasaña and Chueca are the creative dining and bar districts.
# La Latina is the traditional tapas neighbourhood (Cava Baja, Cava Alta).
# Lavapiés is the multicultural and emerging food hub.
# Salamanca is the upscale dining district. Chamberí has strong neighbourhood dining.
# Mercado de San Miguel (adjacent to Plaza Mayor) is tourist-facing — apply caution.
# Huertas/Las Letras is the literary district with traditional restaurants.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Malasaña, Chueca, La Latina, Lavapiés, Chamberí
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Salamanca, Retiro, Huertas, Argüelles, Moncloa
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Tetuán, Vallecas, Carabanchel, Hortaleza, Usera
