GEOGRAPHY
════════════════════════════════════════

No known toponym collision for Madrid. Standard disambiguation is
not required — do not add "Spain" or "España" to every query. Madrid
is unambiguous in food writing searches.

NEIGHBOURHOOD_TIERS — target all three tiers across Phase 0 and
Phase 1 searches:

Tier 1 (high food writing density):
  Malasaña, Chueca, La Latina

Tier 2 (strong secondary density):
  Lavapiés, Salamanca, Chamberí

Tier 3 (emerging or underrepresented):
  Palacio, Sol, Retiro, Carabanchel, Usera, Tetuán

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_EXCLUDE:
Any restaurant described as "outside Madrid", "Community of Madrid
but not capital", or in named satellite towns (Pozuelo de Alarcón,
Boadilla del Monte, San Lorenzo de El Escorial, Alcobendas, etc.).
Montia (San Lorenzo de El Escorial) is 1★ Michelin but outside the
city — auto-exclude by geography, not by starred status.
