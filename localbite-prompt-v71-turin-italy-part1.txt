EN source gap: Mimi Thorisson (Airmail News, May 2025) is the only
confirmed EN Phase 0 source for Turin. Her guide names specific
restaurants with clear prose context. The pipeline should extract
from this source in Phase 0 before proceeding to Phase 1.

Italian-language sources: Turin has limited dedicated IT food media
compared to Milan or Rome. Phase 1 should search for:
  "migliori ristoranti Torino osterie piole 2024 2025"
  "dove mangiare Torino guida gastronomica 2025"
  "Torino ristoranti consigliati critico gastronomico 2025"
  Named IT Turin food writers and local bloggers

Piedmontese food identity: Turin is the capital of one of Italy's
greatest food regions. The pipeline should target:
  - Piole (traditional Piedmontese wine bars/osterie)
  - Tajarin (thin egg pasta with butter and truffles)
  - Agnolotti del plin (tiny stuffed pasta)
  - Vitello tonnato (cold veal with tuna sauce)
  - Bagna cauda (anchovy and garlic warm dip)
  - Bicerin (traditional Turin hot drink — coffee, chocolate, cream)
  - Natural wine bars in the Quadrilatero Romano
  - Porta Palazzo market area restaurants

Turin food culture note: Multiple food writers (including Mimi
Thorisson) have noted that finding great food in Turin is harder
than expected given the quality of the surrounding region. This
reinforces the value of a curated LocalBite pack over generic
searches. Prioritise sources that have deep local knowledge.

Both-pool requirements: Mimi Thorisson (EN, Airmail News) + any
qualifying IT source from Phase 1 would form a both-pool pair.
Different publishers, different languages — qualifies.
