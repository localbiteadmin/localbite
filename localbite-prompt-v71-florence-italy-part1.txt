Italian-language sources: Florence has strong IT food media.
Phase 1 should search for:
  - Named Italian food critics and bloggers covering Florence
  - Leonardo Romanelli (leonardoromanelli.it) — check Phase 0 first
  - Dissapore, Gambero Rosso blog (flag COI), local Florence food media
  - Florentine food blogs in Italian

Florentine food identity: Bistecca alla Fiorentina, ribollita, pappa
al pomodoro, lampredotto (tripe sandwiches), crostini di fegatini.
Emiko Davies explicitly recommends seeking out lampredotto, trattorie
in Sant'Ambrogio market area, and wine bars in Oltrarno. These are
the kind of restaurants the pipeline should prioritise — places locals
eat, not tourist-facing restaurants near Ponte Vecchio.

Overtourism note: Emiko Davies has written extensively about how
Florence's restaurant scene is pressured by mass tourism. Restaurants
explicitly recommended by named writers as "local" or "away from
tourists" should be prioritised. Avoid extracting restaurants that
writers flag as tourist traps.

Both-pool requirements: Emiko Davies (EN/emikodavies.substack.com) and
any IT source from Phase 1 would form a both-pool pair if different
publishers and different languages. If Leonardo Romanelli (IT) confirms
as a qualifying source in Phase 0, the pipeline has immediate both-pool
potential.

Fiesole exclusion: Any restaurant described as being "in Fiesole",
"above Florence in Fiesole", or "overlooking Florence from Fiesole" is
outside the city bounding box and must be excluded regardless of writer
recommendation. This includes Coquinarius Fiesole and Serrae Villa
Fiesole (1★ Michelin — also excluded on Michelin grounds).
