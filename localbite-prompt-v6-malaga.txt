════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 2 — Assign confidence tier

TIER A — Auto-approve:
- 2 or more independent sources, OR
- Both-pool: ≥1 EN source AND ≥1 ES source, editorially
  independent AND from different publishers.
  NOTE: Same-publisher cross-language editions (e.g. Time Out EN
  and Time Out ES) do NOT qualify as independent agreement and
  do NOT produce a both-pool entry.

TIER B — Review recommended:
Primary single-source:
- Good fetch AND quote ≥15 words AND ≥1 specific detail

Secondary or conflicted single-source:
- Good fetch AND quote ≥15 words AND ≥2 specific details

TIER C — Auto-reject:
- Partial/failed fetch, single source
- Quote under 15 words or no specific details
- Outside geographic boundary

STEP 2B — PUBLISHER CONCENTRATION CHECK
After tier assignment:

For each unique publisher count:
- Total articles from this publisher in Tier A + Tier B
- Percentage of each language pool held by this publisher

pool's sources:

  [Y]% of [language] pool

- If still not met after 4 searches: document as structural
