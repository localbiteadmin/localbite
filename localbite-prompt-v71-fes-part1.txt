# ── NEIGHBOURHOOD STRUCTURE ──────────────────────────────────
# Fes has two historic medinas plus the French-built Ville Nouvelle.
# Fes el-Bali is the UNESCO-listed ancient medina — the core dining area.
# Fes el-Jdid is the smaller adjoining medina with the mellah (Jewish quarter).
# Ville Nouvelle (Agdal/Ziat) has modern restaurants and cafes.
# Riad restaurants are common — apply RIAD_RULE.

TIER_1_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Fes el-Bali, Fes el-Jdid, Ville Nouvelle
TIER_2_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Agdal, Ziat, Dhar Mehraz
TIER_3_NEIGHBOURHOODS: Aïn Chkef, Saïss

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_INCLUDE:
  Fes el-Bali (entire ancient medina), Fes el-Jdid, Mellah,
  Ville Nouvelle, Agdal, Ziat, Dhar Mehraz

GEOGRAPHIC_BOUNDARY_EXCLUDE:
  Meknès (separate city 60km away), Ifrane, Azrou,
