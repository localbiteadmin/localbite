Triestine food identity: Trieste has a unique food culture
shaped by its Habsburg history and position on the border
of Italy, Slovenia, and the Adriatic. The pipeline should target:
  - Buffet triestino (traditional Triestine buffet restaurants
    serving boiled meats, sauerkraut, musetto — a local tradition
    unique to Trieste)
  - Osmize (seasonal farmhouse wine bars on the Carso plateau —
    only open a few weeks per year each, but a defining institution)
  - Jota (bean and sauerkraut soup)
  - Boreto alla graisana (Grado-style fish stew)
  - Caffè storici (historic cafés — Caffè San Marco, Caffè degli
    Specchi — coffee culture is integral to Triestine identity)
  - Central European influences: goulash, strudel, Wiener Schnitzel

Multilingual note: Trieste has Slovenian-speaking communities
and some food writers may cover the city in Slovenian. Italian
and English are the target source languages per pipeline rules.

Both-pool requirements: No Phase 0 sources confirmed. Phase 1
must find qualifying EN and IT sources. If fewer than 10R can
be reached, document as thin city and park.
