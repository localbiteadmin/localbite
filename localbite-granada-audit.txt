STRUCTURAL NOTES
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Granada thin-source-pool: This city has very few qualifying food writers
covering the dining scene in 2023-2026. Primary ES coverage is extremely
limited — GranadaDigital (Cloudflare-blocked), ideal.es (not surfacing
relevant food writing), El Comidista/El País (no Granada-specific articles).
The thin source pool combined with strict concentration cap enforcement
produces a 4-restaurant dataset. This is a structural characteristic of
Granada's English/Spanish food writing ecosystem, not a pipeline failure.

WebFetch truncation impact: The Jina/WebFetch fetch pathway abbreviates
quotes during AI content processing. This caused Taberna La Tana and Café
4 Gatos (from Sabores extraction) to fall below the 15-word minimum
threshold. Taberna La Tana (cross-validated rank 3) was lost entirely.
This is a known pipeline limitation; results are conservative relative to
a human extraction of the same sources.

GranadaDigital (El Gocho Gourmet) note: Column launched April 2025;
Cloudflare permanently blocks Jina fetcher. Recommended for monitoring
in future pipeline runs as content depth grows.
